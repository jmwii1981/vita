Leaning into the hustle

I am just as passionate about the quality of a project as I am about getting that project launched. And to hit those targets, I believe in effective planning, teamwork, humility, vulnerability, a listening ear, and offering my team the resources that they need to be successful.

I believe that keeping up with industry trends through comparative analysis, keeping my team informed, leading to the tool, and offering a hand when needed has helped me to connect with them on a deeper level. Afterall, we don't hire smart people to tell them what to do. We offer them the inspiration and tools they need to be successful in their journey. When it comes to culture, I come to the office to play hard, get things done, and have a little fun while doing it! Happy people are productive people, so my stance on culture is firm.
